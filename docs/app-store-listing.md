## Long Description

**Stop guessing. Start selling.**

AdAngle AI uses advanced artificial intelligence to analyze your products and discover unique advertising angles that resonate with your target audience. Generate compelling ad copy in seconds, not hours.

### 🎯 Key Features

**AI-Powered Angle Discovery**
Our AI analyzes your product from multiple perspectives, identifying unique selling angles you may have never considered. Each angle targets a specific audience with a clear pain point, emotional hook, and objection handler.

**Multi-Model Copy Generation**
Generate 5 unique ad copies for each angle using multiple AI models (Claude, GPT-4o, Llama, Mixtral). More variety means more opportunities to find what resonates.

**Video Script Generator**
Create engaging video ad scripts optimized for platforms like TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts.

**Hook Variations (Pro & Unlimited)**
Get 5 different scroll-stopping hook variations for each angle to A/B test your ads.

**Multi-Language Support (Unlimited)**
Generate content in 7 languages: English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, and Dutch.

**Bulk Generation (Unlimited)**
Analyze your entire product catalog at once and generate angles for all products simultaneously.
