Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

AdAngle AI ("we", "our", or "us") operates the AdAngle AI application for Shopify. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

1. Information We Collect

When you install AdAngle AI, we collect:

  • Store Information: Your Shopify store domain and basic store details necessary to provide our services.
  • Product Data: Product titles, descriptions, prices, and images that you choose to analyze with our AI tools.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app, including features accessed and AI generations performed.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for:

  • Providing and maintaining our Service
  • Generating AI-powered advertising angles and copy for your products
  • Processing payments and managing subscriptions
  • Improving our Service and developing new features
  • Communicating with you about updates or support

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on servers hosted by Railway. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • OpenRouter: To process AI requests. Product data sent to AI models is used solely for generating your requested content and is not stored by OpenRouter.
  • Shopify: For authentication and billing through the Shopify platform.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Uninstall the app at any time through Shopify

7. GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we comply with GDPR requirements. We process data based on your consent when you install and use our app.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

9. Contact Us
